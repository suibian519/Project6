Mission

The mission of this site is to provide user with a viable means for expanding their musical repertoire. The site supplies orchestras with various tools for grading and searching for pieces of music. The site will strive to connect orchestra conductors and players with pieces of music that suit their skill level and interest.

By Signing Up You Can:

  • Contribute to the archive of previously graded pieces.
  • Customize your personal preferences to include instruments you are familiar with.
  • Create your own orchestra.
  • Grade your musicians.
  • Receive pieces tailored to the skill level of your players.

Grading Explained

The grading scheme allows for grades ranging from 1 to 10. A grade 1 corresponds to a low difficulty part, whereas a 10 corresponds to a very difficult part.

Example Cello Grading:

  • Grade 1: Benjamin Britten ripieno for St Nicholas
  • Grade 2: Bizet Farandole from L'Arlesienne Suite
  • Grade 3: Corelli ripieno for any of the concerti grossi
  • Grade 4: Schubert 1st movement of the Unfinished Symphony
  • Grade 5: Beethoven 1st movement from 1st Symphony
  • Grade 6: Beethoven 2nd and 3rd movements from 5th Symphony
  • Grade 7: Shostakovitch Festival Overture
  • Grade 8: Mozart Magic Flute Overture
  • Grade 9: Richard Strauss solo part for Don Quixote
  • Grade 10: Haydn solo part from the Symphonia Concertante
Note:

Grades 1-8 are similar to the grades for the ABRSM exams, grade 9 is at the level of a teaching diploma, and grade 10 a performance diploma. (ABRSM is the exam board of the Royal Schools of Music).
